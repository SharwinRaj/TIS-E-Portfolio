# Industry Talk 1: PPG Business & IT Landscape

## Description
I attended Industry Talk 1: PPG Business & IT Landscape as part of the Technology and Information System course at Universiti Teknologi Malaysia. The session focused on how large organisations utilise information technology to support business operations at an enterprise level.

The speaker highlighted the importance of data analytics, cloud infrastructure, and SAP systems in ensuring efficient and reliable business processes.

## Reflection
This talk provided valuable insights into the responsibilities of ICT professionals in large organisations. I learned that technical skills must be complemented with teamwork, problem ownership, and continuous learning in order to maintain complex information systems effectively.
